Wide shot from above, camera rapidly cranes down as three giant watermelon crates are violently jolted up from the truck bed, tumbling freely towards the crowded market below. The Dutch angle and rapid downward motion of the camera emphasize the sudden danger. A small blue hedgehog boy clutching the back of the truck snaps his gaze upward, his grip loosening as his eyes fly wide open in shock and a sharp breath catches in his throat.

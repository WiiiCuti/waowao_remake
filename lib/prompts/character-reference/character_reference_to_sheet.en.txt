# Reference Image to Character Sheet Prompt (img2img Mode)

Based on the provided reference image, extract the character's facial features, hairstyle, body type, and clothing style as reference.

## Art Style Priority Rules

The art style is determined by the user-selected style instruction; strictly follow the style instruction for generation.
The reference image is only used to preserve the character's identity traits (facial features, hairstyle, body type, clothing structure) and must not override the user-specified art style.
Only when no style instruction is provided may the original image's style be referenced.

## Generation Rules

1. Ignore the original image's specific color tones and lighting
2. Use natural, soft studio lighting
3. Render normal, aesthetically pleasing body proportions
4. Do not copy the original image's image quality, blur, noise, or defects
5. The generated image must be clear and sharp, rich in detail, professional quality
6. The character's expression should be a naturally calm neutral expression, gaze looking straight at the camera

## Missing Body Part Auto-Completion

If the reference image is a half-body or partial body shot, reasonably complete the unexposed body parts based on the clothing style and character traits:
- **Missing lower body**: infer and render matching pants/skirt based on the top style
- **Missing feet**: add suitable footwear based on the overall outfit style
- **Missing hands/arms**: reasonably complete based on the pose
- Maintain overall style consistency, ensuring the completed parts are harmoniously coordinated with the visible parts
